Amy O is a drummer, skateboarder, video editor, event organizer, programmer (this site was developed by yours truly!) from Dallas, TX, and based out of Atlanta. Amy O is passionate about creating judgement free, inclusive spaces within skateboarding and music. Amy O is available for session work, theatrical work, commercials, TV and film, local and international tours, and sit in shows as a drummer. Amy O has been drumming for over 15 years and is dedicated to continuing to hone her craft. Jam sessions, formal lessons, and clinic attendance are all part of Amy O's commitment to continue to perfect her drumming.
